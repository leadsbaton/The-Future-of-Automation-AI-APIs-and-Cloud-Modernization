Succeeding with Modern Data Applications

Data apps – from in-product dashboards to recommendation engines – are essential for driving data-informed decisions across organizations. They help users quickly extract key trends, insights, and actions from massive datasets, improving productivity, operational efficiency, and innovation.

However, building effective data apps isn't easy. Beyond handling large-scale data processing, apps must be fast, often delivering real-time or near-real-time insights. They also need to support high concurrency, serving many users and requests simultaneously. Integration and governance are critical too; data must be accurate, timely, and well-managed. Legacy systems, data silos, and poor data quality can all be obstacles to success.
